About NEET

NEET is a fun token that represents the spirit of those who are not currently in employment, education, or training. It aims to create a community of like-minded individuals who embrace the NEET lifestyle.

With NEET, you can participate in various activities, including meme contests, community events, and more. Our mission is to spread awareness and create a fun environment for everyone!

Join us as we embark on this exciting journey together!
